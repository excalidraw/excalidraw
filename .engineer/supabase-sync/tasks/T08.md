### Popover approach
**Custom absolutely-positioned `<div>` toggled by `useState(open)`** with an outside-pointerdown listener to
close (not the package `Popover`). Reason: the package `Popover` needs numeric `top`/`left` anchoring +
viewport math (designed for canvas-anchored pickers); a self-contained relative/absolute card is
dependency-light, fully presentational, and trivially testable for T11. Styled via SyncStatusButton.scss.
